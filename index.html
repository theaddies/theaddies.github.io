My name is Andrew Addie. I am a father of 2 teenage daughters
who are both at university. I was born in Melbourne, Australia
and moved to the southern United States at the age of 8.
I graduate from Georgia Tech with a bachelors degree in
Chemical Engineering and have a Master's degree also in Chemical
Engineering from the University of Delaware. I have traveled
extensively around the world and lived in Italy for 1 year and
England for 3 years.

My list of hobbies are quite lengthy. Currently I am interested
in backpacking and camping. I am also spending a lot of time
learning to program in my data analysis bootcamp with Case Western
Reserve University. Other hobbies that I have enjoyed in the past
are electronics, computer networking, woodworking, biking and bike
touring and running.
